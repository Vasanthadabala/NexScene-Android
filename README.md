## 🚀 Overview

NexScene is a modern Movie & TV Show Tracker built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, designed for users who love discovering and organizing movies and series.

From building watchlists to tracking watched content and finding streaming platforms, NexScene makes your entertainment experience seamless and enjoyable.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔎 Discover Movies & Series
- Explore trending, popular, and upcoming movies and TV shows from around the world.

### 📌 Watchlist Management
- Save movies and shows to your personal watchlist so you never forget what to watch next.

### ⭐ Rate & Track
- Rate the movies and series you've watched and keep a personal history of your viewing activity.

### 📺 Where to Watch
- Find out which streaming platforms offer your favorite movies and series.

### 🎞 Detailed Movie & Show Info
- View complete details including:
      - Posters and images
      - Ratings
      - Overview
      - Release information
      - Cast and trailers

### 🔍 Smart Search
- Quickly search for any movie or TV show and explore detailed information instantly.

### 📺 Where to Watch
- Find out which streaming platforms offer your favorite movies and series.

### 🎨 Beautiful UI
- A clean and modern UI built with Jetpack Compose, designed for a smooth and enjoyable experience.

## TMDB API key setup
Add your key in `local.properties`:

```properties
TMDB_API_KEY=your_tmdb_api_key_here
```

`local.properties` is already gitignored, so this key will not be pushed to GitHub.
